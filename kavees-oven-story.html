The Beginning

How It All Started

It started with a simple birthday cake — baked for a cousin's surprise party with no professional training, no fancy equipment, just heart, flour, and an old family recipe. The look on the birthday girl's face when she saw it was all the motivation Kavee needed.

Word spread through family and friends. One cake became ten, ten became a hundred. What started as a weekend hobby slowly became Sri Lanka's most trusted home bakery.

"I never planned to start a business. I just loved making people smile. The cakes came after."
— Kavee, Founder

Today, Kavee's Oven serves hundreds of families across Sri Lanka — from intimate birthday celebrations to grand wedding receptions — always with the same warmth and care as that very first cake.

Our Journey

From Humble Beginnings

Every milestone baked with love — a story of passion growing one cake at a time.

2018 · The First Cake
A Cousin's Birthday Changed Everything

Kavee baked her very first custom cake for a family celebration. The vanilla sponge with handmade cream roses was an instant hit — and the seed of something magical was planted.

First Custom Cake
2019 · Growing by Word of Mouth
The First 50 Orders

With no advertising, just word of mouth, Kavee received her first 50 orders within a year. Neighbours, colleagues, and strangers were discovering that home-baked really does taste different.

50+ Customers
2020 · Going Digital
Instagram & WhatsApp Orders Begin

During the pandemic, Kavee launched her Instagram page showcasing her creations. Overnight the response was overwhelming — orders poured in from across Colombo and beyond.

2,000 Followers
2021 · First Wedding Cake
A Three-Tier Dream Come True

Kavee baked her first wedding cake — a three-tier rose gold fondant masterpiece. The photo went viral on Instagram and marked the beginning of Kavee's Oven's reputation for premium occasion cakes.

Wedding Specialist
2023 · Island-Wide Delivery
Taking Kavee's Oven Across Sri Lanka

Expanded delivery island-wide. Customers from Kandy, Galle, Negombo and beyond could now receive fresh cakes at their doorstep — still packed with the same love as always.

Island-Wide Delivery
2025 · Today
500+ Families & Still Growing

Today Kavee's Oven serves over 500 happy families, has baked over 1,200 cakes, and holds a perfect 5-star rating. The journey continues — one cake, one smile, one memory at a time.
