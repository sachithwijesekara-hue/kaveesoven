FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find your answer? Send us a message or WhatsApp us directly.

How far in advance do I need to order?
Custom and decorated cakes require a minimum of 48 hours notice. For same-day orders, call us directly on 076-XXX-XXXX and we'll confirm availability. Wedding and large event cakes should be ordered at least 2 weeks in advance.
Do you deliver island-wide?
Yes! We deliver across Sri Lanka. Delivery is FREE for orders above LKR 5,000 within Colombo and surrounding areas. For outstation deliveries, a fee applies based on distance — WhatsApp us for a quote.
Can I request a fully custom design?
Absolutely! We specialise in fully custom cakes. Share a photo, describe your idea, or let us suggest something. We can do character cakes, photo cakes, floral designs, corporate branding, and more. Send us a message with your inspiration!
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Credit / Debit Cards (via PayHere), Bank Transfer, and Cash on Delivery. For WhatsApp orders we'll share our bank details directly. A 50% deposit may be required for large custom orders.
Do you make eggless cakes?
Yes! Our Eggless Delight range is baked fresh daily and has the same great taste and texture as our regular cakes. Just mention "eggless" when placing your order and we'll make sure it's prepared accordingly.
What is your cancellation / refund policy?
Cancellations made 48+ hours before the delivery date receive a full refund. Cancellations within 48 hours may be subject to a 50% charge as ingredients will already have been sourced. Please contact us as early as possible if plans change.
How are cakes packaged?
All cakes are packaged in sturdy, branded boxes designed to keep them safe during transport. Tiered wedding cakes are assembled on-site if required. We also offer gift wrapping for cupcakes and brownie boxes.
How do I leave a review?
We'd love to hear from you! You can leave a review through our contact form above, on our Facebook page, or via Google Reviews. Your feedback helps us improve and helps other customers discover us!
